Recofes - 2k25

Unleash Your Talent... Celebrate the Vibe... Recofes-2K25 is an exciting intra-college non-technical cultural fest hosted over two action-packed days, featuring a wide range of events and performances. It's the perfect platform to showcase your creativity, express your passion, and experience unforgettable moments with your peers. From music and dance to art and drama, there's something for everyone. Get ready to be inspired, entertained, and celebrated. Join us and make memories that last a lifetime!
